Search the Lobbyist Registry

This portion of the City of Toronto Website is being used for the purpose of disclosure of information contained in the City's Lobbyist Registry System (the "System") under Chapter 140, Lobbying, of the City of Toronto Municipal Code. This site is being made available to allow members of the public to readily obtain information regarding the activities of lobbyists involved in lobbying the City of Toronto.
